# üöÄ **Fast and Informed Pathfinding with A * Search Algorithm**

Google Maps revolutionized digital navigation ‚Äî and behind that speed and accuracy is the **A\*** (**A-star**) Search Algorithm. A* blends the precision of Dijkstra's algorithm with the intuition of heuristics (like straight-line distance) to guide users from Point A to Point B as efficiently as possible.

Whether it's cars on the road, drones in the sky, or even queries in a data graph ‚Äî A* helps Google solve **shortest path** problems with speed and intelligence.

---

## üß≠ Graph-Based Map Representation

- **Nodes:**  
  Intersections, points of interest, or GPS coordinates.

- **Edges:**  
  Roads, paths, or transit connections with associated weights (time, distance, traffic delay).

- **Heuristic (h(n)):**  
  Often the **Euclidean distance** or **time estimate** to the destination ‚Äî helps A* prioritize better candidates.

- **Cost Function:**  
  > **f(n) = g(n) + h(n)**  
  - **g(n):** Actual cost from start to current node  
  - **h(n):** Heuristic cost from current to goal

---

## üéØ Objective

To **find the shortest or fastest route** in a massive and dynamic network ‚Äî in milliseconds ‚Äî using **informed pathfinding** that considers:

- Traffic  
- Road conditions  
- Blockages  
- Travel mode (walk, drive, cycle, transit)

---

## ‚öôÔ∏è Algorithm Used

A* traverses the graph by expanding the node with the **lowest estimated total cost (f)**. It combines the thoroughness of Dijkstra with the efficiency of heuristics.

### ‚úÖ Benefits

- Blazing-fast route computation  
- Prioritized search reduces unnecessary expansion  
- Adapts well to real-world constraints (live traffic, accidents, etc.)

| Metric             | Value              | Description                                      |
|--------------------|--------------------|--------------------------------------------------|
| ‚è≥ Time Complexity  | `O(b^d)`            | With `b` as branching factor and `d` as depth   |
| üß† Space Complexity | `O(b^d)`            | Needs to store frontier (priority queue)         |
